A novel actuation principle.

Bezalel introduces a novel actuation principle where a small set of actuators controls an entire grid of pins. Two orthogonal groups of actuators push or pull an array of rotary elements, efficiently converting their motion into independent pin actuation.

Scalability and Efficiency

Traditional tactile displays are expensive because each pin usually needs its own actuator — making cost grow quadratically with resolution.

By using a novel actuation principle, Bezalel scales linearly. This makes high-resolution tactile displays a reality for independent makers and small labs.

Applications

The Bezalel platform enables a new class of tactile interfaces, from tangible dashboards for AI agents to inclusive educational games.

  • 3×3 AI Interaction Matrix: A ready-to-use, 3D-printable dashboard for physicalizing digital models.
  • Inclusive Play Packs: Tactile simulators for games like Tic-Tac-Toe and beyond.
  • Future Horizons: Scaling to 6×6 high-resolution surfaces for spatial AI visualization.
